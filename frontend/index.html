🌾 Crop Yield Model

Uses a RandomForestRegressor pipeline with 200 decision trees. Features include OneHotEncoded categorical variables (Crop, Season, State) and numerical inputs (Area, Annual Rainfall, Fertilizer, Pesticide).

📈 Crop Price Forecast Model

Powered by a RandomForestRegressor combined with OrdinalEncoding and temporal lag features (`Price_Lag_1`, `Price_Lag_7`, and `Rolling_Mean_7`) derived from historical mandi arrival data.
